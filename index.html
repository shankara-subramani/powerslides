PowerSlides

Simple markup to make apple-like presentations

Get it on Github: https://github.com/shankariyerr/powerslides

Made with love, by Shankar.

Features

Simple markup, each <div> in the body is a slide. That's it!

Use keyboard shortcuts for navigation (← / →)

Slide Numbers on top

Why? -- Glad that you asked

We at NFN Labs meet every Friday to talk about stuff we think everyone should know.

We try to keep ourselves constantly updated.

This is an attempt to minimise the time taken to make slides for their friday talks
